Welcome to China

China has a mix of ancient cultures, natural wonders, and stunning modern cities.

Beijing

Beijing is famous for the Great Wall and the Forbidden City. It is the historical and cultural center of China.

Shanghai

Shanghai is a modern city with skyscrapers, The Bund, and amazing nightlife.

Guilin

Guilin is famous for its karst-shaped mountains and beautiful scenery along the Li River.
